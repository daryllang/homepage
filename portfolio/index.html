Blogging

Shutterstock Blog (2010 to 2014)

I launched the Shutterstock Blog in 2010, and oversaw a team of staff writers and freelance contributors. Under my leadership, the Shutterstock Blog expanded into 4 international markets and won the 2014 Digital Content Marketing Award for Best Blog.

PDNPulse and PDN Photo of the Day (2005 to 2009)

From 2005 to 2009 I was an editor at PDN, a leading trade magazine for photographers. I launched and edited the PDNPulse photo news blog and PDN Photo of the Day blog, both of which drew hundreds of thousands of monthly unique visitors. I launched and oversaw the @pdnonline Twitter feed, which became an influential source of photography news. While I was its editor, PDNPulse won the 2009 Jesse H. Neal National Business Journalism Award for Best Blog.
